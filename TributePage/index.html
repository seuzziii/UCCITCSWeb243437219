Sanketsu Shoujo Sayuri

The symbol of self-discovery even in isolation and uncertainty

About

Sayuri is known for her numerous J-pop and rock hits, raw acoustic guitar talent, and a hauntingly beautiful connection to a generation grappling with isolation and uncertainty.

Achievements

  • 1996 - Born on June 7, 1996 at Shika Island in Higashi-ku, Fukuoka.
  • 2012 - Youngest winner of the Grand Prix at the 5th Musical Revolution Finals.
  • 2015 - Held her first solo concert, "Yoake no Shitaku", at Tsutaya O-East in Tokyo.
  • 2016 - Made the ending theme of the series, "Erased", which broadened her audience.
  • 2022 - Released ending theme for the show, "Lycoris Recoil", her most streamed track.
  • 2024 - Marriage, chronic illness, and tragic passing at the age of 28.
